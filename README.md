# Hit the Road, Chick!

3D platformer where you play as a brave character trying to collect eggs while avoiding a mischievous cat! Navigate through colorful environments, gather all the eggs, and escape before the cat catches you.

 Gameplay Features
 
Third-person 3D movement with smooth camera controls
Egg collection mechanics - Find and collect all eggs to win
Cat AI chase system - Stay alert! The cat is always hunting you
Health system - Avoid getting caught or lose health
Timer challenge - Complete levels as fast as possible
Colorful cartoon-style graphics with optimized lighting
Settings menu with music/sound controls (Press ESC to open)

 Controls
 
WASD - Move your character
Mouse - Look around and control camera
ESC - Open/close settings menu
Collect eggs by walking into them
Avoid the cat at all costs!

 Audio
 
Background music and sound effects included
Toggle music and sounds in the settings menu
Immersive audio experience
